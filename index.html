What is RPM?

The Center for Medicare and Medicaid (CMS) has finalized billing codes effective January 1st, 2020 which allows physicians to remotely monitor Medicare patients and be reimbursed. Fore more information, please see our brochure.

?

What are the billing codes for RPM?

CPT 99453, 99454, 99457, and 99458 are newly added to reimburse various RPM activities. For more information, please see our brochure.

?

Do patients need to configure the devices?

No configuration is needed at all. Our devices use cellular network, so patients do not need to connect to their Wi-Fi routers. All patients need to do is putting the batteries in, and the devices are ready to use.

?

Can I use Telehealth as a stand-alone solution?

Yes, you can. To use our Telehealth platform without our RPM platform, please contact our sales.
